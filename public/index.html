The Professors are here!

This app adds two slash-comands, /frinkiac and /morbotron, to your Slack. If you know about Frinkiac.com and Morbotron.com, you can guess what these commands do. If not... well, you'll figure it out.

Privacy policy

These slash commands don't collect any private information, as near as we can tell. Nevertheless, we won't use or share such information in any context.
